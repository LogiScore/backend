Our Core Principles

Transparency & Honesty

We believe in honest, transparent reviews that help the logistics community make informed decisions. Every review should reflect genuine experiences.

Anonymity Protection

We protect the anonymity of reviewers who choose to remain anonymous. We will not reveal identities unless required by law.

Neutral Platform

LogiScore is a neutral platform. We do not take sides in disputes between users and forwarders. We focus on maintaining platform integrity.

Professional Standards

We maintain professional standards in all interactions. Reviews should be constructive and based on actual service experiences.

Our Mission: To provide a trusted platform where shippers can share honest experiences and forwarders can showcase their services transparently.

Review Standards & Requirements

What Makes a Good Review

Required Elements

  • Based on actual service experience within the last 2 years
  • Specific details about the service provided
  • Objective assessment of performance
  • Constructive feedback (both positive and negative)
  • Accurate company and branch information

Recommended Elements

  • Include both pros and cons for balanced perspective
  • Mention specific services used (air freight, sea freight, etc.)
  • Reference communication quality and responsiveness
  • Note any special circumstances or requirements
  • Provide context for your rating

Review Frequency & Limits

One Review Per Branch Per Year

  • Maximum one review per forwarder branch per calendar year
  • Updates to existing reviews replace the previous review
  • Reviews must be for services received within the last 2 years
  • Multiple reviews from same company may be flagged for review

Identity & Verification

Reviewer Identity Options

  • Anonymous: No identifying information shown
  • Company Name: Only company name displayed
  • Verified: Company name with verification badge

Prohibited Content & Behavior

Fake or Misleading Reviews

  • Reviews not based on actual service experience
  • Reviews written by competitors or their employees
  • Reviews from employees of the reviewed company
  • Coordinated review campaigns or vote manipulation
  • Reviews with false information about services

Inappropriate Content

  • Personal attacks or harassment
  • Defamatory statements or false accusations
  • Discriminatory language or hate speech
  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Confidential or proprietary information

Platform Abuse

  • Multiple accounts from same user/company
  • Automated or scripted review submissions
  • Attempting to manipulate scores through coordinated actions
  • Circumventing review limits or restrictions
  • Using VPNs or proxies to hide identity for abuse

Commercial Content

  • Advertising or promotional content
  • Links to external websites or services
  • Contact information or solicitation
  • Reviews primarily promoting other services
Important: Violations of these guidelines may result in review removal, account suspension, or permanent ban from the platform.

Enforcement & Consequences

First Violation

Warning

  • Review removal
  • Email warning
  • Guidelines reminder

Second Violation

30-Day Suspension

  • Account suspended
  • Cannot submit new reviews
  • Existing reviews remain visible

Third Violation

Permanent Ban

  • Account permanently banned
  • Company domain may be blocked
  • Appeal process available

Severe Violations (Immediate Ban)

Zero Tolerance Offenses

  • Coordinated fake review campaigns
  • Harassment or threats
  • Multiple fake accounts
  • Attempting to manipulate scores systematically
  • Sharing confidential information

Investigation Process

How We Investigate Violations

  1. Detection: Automated systems and user reports flag potential violations
  2. Review: Human moderators examine the flagged content
  3. Investigation: We check user patterns, IP addresses, and review history
  4. Decision: Moderators make a determination based on evidence
  5. Action: Appropriate consequence is applied
  6. Notification: User is informed of the decision and appeal process

Appeals Process

How to Appeal

  1. Submit Appeal: Email support@logiscore.net with your case
  2. Include Evidence: Provide relevant documentation and explanation
  3. Review Period: Appeals are reviewed within 7-10 business days
  4. Decision: Final decision communicated via email

What to Include in Your Appeal

  • Clear explanation of why the action was taken in error
  • Evidence supporting your case
  • Any relevant context or circumstances
  • Your contact information
  • Reference to the specific review or action being appealed
Appeal Rights: All users have the right to appeal moderation decisions. We review each appeal carefully and fairly.
